Privacy Policy

PrimePop · QratedSoul LLC · Last updated: March 26, 2026

QratedSoul LLC ("we", "our", or "us") built PrimePop as a mobile game application. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Stored on your device only: Game progress (level, score, settings, purchase status) is saved locally using localStorage. This data never leaves your device. If you enter a display name, it is also stored locally only.

Collected by third-party services: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data. See policies.google.com/privacy. Purchases are processed by Apple or Google — we never receive your payment details.

We do NOT collect: personal information, location data, contacts, photos, payment info, or health data.

2. How We Use Information

Local game data is used solely to save your progress. We do not transmit, sell, or share any data — PrimePop runs entirely on your device with no external servers.

3. Advertising

PrimePop displays ads through Google AdMob (interstitials, rewarded ads, and banners). If you purchase the ad-free unlock, all ads are removed and AdMob is no longer initialized.

Manage ad preferences: iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. Android: Settings → Google → Ads.

4. In-App Purchases

All transactions are processed securely through Apple's App Store or Google Play. We never receive or store your payment details.

5. Children's Privacy

PrimePop is a math-based game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the app does not collect personal information from any user, no special data is gathered from children. Parents can manage ad personalization through device settings.

6. Data Retention & Security

All data is stored locally. Delete it anytime via "Reset All Data" in Settings or by uninstalling the app. Since no data is transmitted externally, there is no risk of data breach from our side.

7. Your Rights

You have full control over your data at all times. Delete all app data through Settings or by uninstalling.

8. Changes & Contact

We may update this policy; changes will be reflected here with an updated date.
